Spark Plug R&R @ 166,362 MILES

The S52 was in need of new spark plugs as during cold starts, I would experience symptoms of misfiring. Though spark plugs may not have been the issue here, it's always nice to do some preventative maintenance and replace them before they cause headaches. Swapped out the old set of NGK BKR6EQUP (Laser Platinum) and replaced with the same type. Top quality OEM+ spark plugs.

Date: March 12, 2026. Mileage: 166,362 mies.
