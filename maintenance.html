Maintenance Log

Inspections

Engine & Drivetrain

  • Engine oil level & conditionCheck level, look for milkiness or metal particles
  • Coolant level & conditionCheck expansion tank; inspect for rust or oil contamination
  • Drive belts — serpentine / alternator / A/CChecked all belts for cracking, fraying, and tension
  • Accessory belt tensioner condition
  • Throttle body & intake bootInspect for cracks, vacuum leaks, and oil residue
  • Air filter condition
  • Fuel lines & connections — leaks, chafing
  • PCV / CCV systemCheck hoses for cracking; common failure on S52
  • Spark plugsReplaced — NGK BKR6EQUP Laser Platinum, Mar 12 2026
  • Ignition coil packs — condition & secure fitment
  • Engine mounts — visual check for cracks
  • Transmission fluid level (ZF 5-speed)
  • Differential fluid level & leaks

Brakes

  • Brake pad thickness — front & rear
  • Rotor condition — thickness, scoring, warping
  • Brake fluid level & colorShould be clear/light yellow — dark means moisture saturation
  • Brake lines & hoses — leaks, cracking, chafing
  • Caliper condition & slide pins
  • Parking brake function & cable adjustment

Suspension & Steering

  • Front struts — leaks, condition
  • Rear shocks — leaks, condition
  • Control arm bushings — front & rear
  • Ball joints — play, condition
  • Tie rod ends — inner & outer
  • Sway bar end links & bushings
  • Subframe mounts — cracking (known E36 issue)
  • Power steering fluid level & lines
  • Steering rack — play, leaks

Electrical & Lighting

  • Battery voltage & terminals — corrosion
  • Alternator output
  • All exterior lights — headlights, tails, fogs, brake, reverse
  • Turn signals & hazards
  • Horn
  • OBD fault codes — scan for pending codes

Body, Fluids & Miscellaneous

  • Windshield wiper bladesReplaced — see Wiper R&R entry
  • Washer fluid level
  • Tire tread depth & even wear pattern
  • Tire pressure — all four corners + spare
  • Wheel lug bolt torque
  • Underbody — fluid leaks, rust, damage
  • Door, hood & trunk seals
  • Sunroof drain tubes — clear, not blocked
