Clear Coat Repair

Noticed the beginnings of clear coat failure on the passenger side mirror and a small patch on the trunk lid—common E36 pain points. Decided to address it before the oxidation spread further.

Sanded back the failing layers with 800-grit wet/dry, progressed to 1500, and applied three stages of 2K High-Gloss Clear. The finish isn't 100% factory perfect, but it's protected and looks significantly better than the peeling mess it was before.

Date: March 25, 2026
