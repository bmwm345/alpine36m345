My account of the maintenance performed on my 1998 M3/4/5. Yes, it has 4 doors. No, it's not a 'family car'. In 1995, the E34 M5 was headed for the boneyard and the E39 was still a fever dream in a Munich boardroom. Needing a placeholder that could also give BMW ///M a reputation in the sports car world, BMW gave the E36 M3 two extra doors and a bit of dignity. It worked, as Caranddriver praised it placed first in its division as the most athletic sports car in 1997.
