Optioned by Sandy Sansing BMW of Penascola. The Style 39 wheels pull the sedan’s profile together and complements the body lines well. The real significance is that this was the first M3 to offer four doors. It proved that a practical sedan could still have the heart of a driver’s car and its younger siblings of the ///M lineup follow the same philosophy, even to this day.
