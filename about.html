  • Production: Optioned via Sandy Sansing BMW; rolled off the line Wednesday Sept 17, 1997.
  • Wheel Spec: Factory Style 39 "Sunflowers". Weight: ~22 lbs (Optioned over Style 23 Soccerball Wheels).
  • Production Window: M3 Sedan production spanned Sept 1996 – May 1998, compared to Coupes which were produced from 1992 to 1999.
  • Rarity: Approximately 1,200 M3 Sedans produced for the North American market.
  • Engineering: B-pillar design provides superior rigidity over coupe variants; hence the usage of the sedan body style in DTM racing.
